10 small red potatoes quarted
6 thin carrots sliced lengthwise
1 bag (8 oz) pearl onions peeled
3lbs corn beef with spices

Put potatoes, carrots 1/2 onions in slow cooker
Put 20 ounces water
Rinse corn beef and add, place spices on top
Add the other 1/2 onions
Cook on slow for 10 hrs

After 10hrs, add cabbage (3/4 head slice), cook
for an additional hour but on HIGH
